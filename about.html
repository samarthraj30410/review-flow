Our Story

Building serverless feedback pipelines that keep local storefronts growing without overhead costs.

Who We Are

At ReviewFlo, we design modern, minimalist review collection tools specifically for local storefronts, cafés, salons, clinics, and gyms. We believe that small businesses shouldn't have to pay high recurring monthly fees to maintain a private database and collect feedback.

Our mission is simple: to configure, brand, and launch high-performance static websites that log customer feedback directly to secure, private spreadsheets.

Why Serverless?

Traditional feedback systems rely on complex databases and active host instances, costing business owners up to $50–$100 every single month just for server uptime. By utilizing serverless edge technologies (Vercel CDN, Cloudflare Workers, and Google Sheets API), we route review payloads instantaneously and safely without server databases.

This design operates entirely on third-party free hosting tiers, providing local storefronts with a zero-maintenance model that costs nothing to keep online.

Our Core Values

Performance

Sub-second page load speeds built via static delivery systems, ensuring your customers never walk away from leaving a review due to slow performance.

Privacy-First

Customer submissions bypass centralized third-party servers and stream directly to your business spreadsheet, guaranteeing ownership and user privacy.

No Overhead

By designing on globally cached edge templates, we eliminate monthly database fees completely, transferring full code ownership directly to you.
