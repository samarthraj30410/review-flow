2. Description of Service

ReviewFlow specializes in the design, construction, and deployment of serverless customer feedback and review systems for local storefronts. Our service workflow includes:

  • Registration of a custom brand-relevant domain through GoDaddy (or other registrars).
  • Configuration of nameservers and edge proxy rules using Cloudflare.
  • Setup of serverless redirection and template routing on Vercel and GitHub.
  • Integration of direct feedback pipelines logging to customer-owned Google Sheets via Apps Script.

3. Account Registration & Domains

To facilitate the setup of your custom review pipeline, ReviewFlow registers custom domains and Gmail accounts on your behalf.

Upon receipt of full payment, full ownership credentials of the registered Gmail address, GoDaddy domain account (including nameservers), and Vercel hosting projects are transferred directly to you. It is the client's sole responsibility to maintain payment renewals for the GoDaddy domain name after the initial acquisition period has expired.

ReviewFlow is not responsible for any such fees.
