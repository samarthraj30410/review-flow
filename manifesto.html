The ReviewFlo Manifesto

Software should be beautiful, fast, and completely free from artificial rent-seeking.

1. Zero Server Overhead

We believe that small businesses should not be paying endless subscriptions for idle servers. By routing directly to Google Sheets and building on serverless edge networks, we eliminate the traditional database overhead, transferring those savings directly back to the business owner.

2. Data Ownership and Privacy

Your customers' data belongs to you. Period. We don't cache it, we don't hold it hostage, and we don't sell it. Customer feedback bypasses arbitrary middlemen, landing instantly and securely in your private domain. You have absolute control.

3. Aesthetic Excellence

A review portal should reflect the brand's quality. We reject generic, uninspired templates in favor of bespoke, highly-crafted visual experiences. Every interaction should feel premium, engaging, and alive.

4. Frictionless Experience

The best UI is the one that gets out of the user's way. Our interfaces are designed to be lightning-fast and universally accessible. No mandatory sign-ups, no bloated JavaScript, just a direct path from customer sentiment to recorded data.
